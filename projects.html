I am part of the Mars Rover Project team at Cornell University! I'm currently on the software subteam, where we focus on path-planning, computer vision, and controls for the rover. More specifically, I'm working with a partner on the user interface for the rover. In the past, we've struggled to efficiently see information of the rover while its out in the field, so we're trying to make the process of visualizing the rover's information much simpler. We are using Foxglove Studio, a robot visualization software, and React.js to customize our own GUI panels. Read more about the team and the competition we participate in on the website below!
